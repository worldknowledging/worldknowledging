    • 10 must read romance books for your 2024 autumn book recommendation list

    Autumn is here, and nothing makes it more special than books.

    After a busy workday or a school-stuffed schedule, all we miss are the cozy vibes of curling up under a blanket, wearing pajamas, sipping hot tea, and diving into the world of books."

    Here are 10 fall books for you to start your fall season and end the year with cozy, autumn vibes.

    Add this to your 2024 autumn book recommendation list.

    1.Clean point:

    A spicy enemies-to-lovers romance about Scottie, a tennis player who was abused by her father, who is also a tennis player himself. She later partners with his rival, Nico, against her father and finds a chance at love.

    Buy on Amazon at 22$

    2.Sunlight:

    "I hate my boss. Like, loathe him. Two years ago, he semi-broke my heart and he still doesn’t remember who I am. Okay so I was covered in electric blue body paint, and wearing a wig, but is that a good excuse to not remember the girl you said was your destiny?"

    When your boss uses you at a business dinner, pretending you're his girlfriend, all while ignoring the fact that one day, this wasn't just a show—he was actually your boyfriend.

    Buy on Amazon at 13$

    3.Is she really going out with him:

    Columnist Anna Appleby has left her love life behind after a painful divorce.Now she's not intrested in a love story at all.All she needs is to take care of her two beloved kids and cat.

    Besides, she’d rather be single than subject herself to the hell of online dating.

    But her office rival is vying for her column, and no column means no stable source of income. In a desperate attempt to keep her job, Anna finds herself pitching a unique angle: seven dates, all found offline, chosen by her children.

    When Love appears unexpectdly in the very unpredictible place , will she be brave to enroll in a new relationship this time.

    Buy on Amazon at 17$

    4.Best hex ever:

    A fantasy romance book that gives cozy and witchy vibes.

    Dina is a skilled kitchen witch, but not an expert in romance. She finds herself in a contemporary love story, where love and fear interfere.

    Buy on Amazon at 18$

    5.Cash delgado is living the dream:

    Cash Delgado is a bartender in a small town.Her six years old sister keeps life intresting and her best fried Inez.

    Cash starts having intimate dreams about Inez that will likely lead her heart to endless possibilities.

    Buy on Amazon at 16$

    6.A legend in the baking:

    "August Hodges, a silent cupcake shop partner, unexpectedly goes viral on social media. Sloane Dell, his best friend’s little sister, is hired as the shop’s social media manager.

    But she was also his crush a long time ago.

    Will love be given a chance? Will their hearts open for it?

    Buy on Amazon at 12$

    7.Haunt your heart out:

    Lex McCall is a worker in a bookstore in a small Vermont town who gets involved in a romantic and comical situation with James,

    a ghost hunter who's part of a documentary crew in town to investigate some fake ghost stories Lex shared in a vlog.

    Buy on Amazon at 16$

    8.Given our history:

    Two professors with a complicated past relationship try to prove history wrong when given a chance to love.

    Buy on Amazon at 16$

    Daydream:(The maple Hills Series book 3)

    Henry Turner is a college junior and the captain of the hockey team. He is struggling with a difficult class and a strict professor.

    On his journey, he accidentally befriends Halle Jacobs, a gifted fellow student, after crashing her book club.

    She is also trying to overcome writer’s block to finish her book. Since both of them need help, they make a deal:

    Henry will improve in his classes, and Halle will gain new experiences that will hopefully reignite her passion for writing.

    Buy on Amazon at 14$

    9.The slowest burn:

    Ellie Wasserman, a ghostwriter for celebrity cookbooks, and Kieran O'Neill, a messy but skilled chef, are working together to create Kieran's cookbook, while their emotions are cooking or maybe close to burning.

    But will they confront their pasts and embrace the future?

    Buy on Amazon at 16$

    10.The seemingly impossible life of Amanda Dean:

    Amanda is minutes away from saying yes to her fiancé on her wedding day, but the doubts and voices inside her can't align with her decision.

    Will she say yes or no?

    And if she says yes, will she be saying no to her feelings and doubts—to herself?

    A beautiful self-discovery Novel who leads nowhere but the truth.

    Buy on Amazon at 19$
